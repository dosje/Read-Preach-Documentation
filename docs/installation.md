# Installation

## Requirements

- **iPhone or iPad**
- **iOS 16.0 or later**
- No internet connection required after installation

## Installing from the App Store

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Search for **"Read & Preach"**
3. Tap **Get**, then authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
4. The app downloads and appears on your home screen

## Updating the app

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Tap your profile icon in the top right corner
3. Scroll down to see available updates
4. Tap **Update** next to Read & Preach

Alternatively, enable automatic updates in **Settings → App Store → App Updates**.

## Transferring data to a new device

Read & Preach stores all data locally. To move your data to a new device:

1. On your **old device**, open Read & Preach → **Settings → Export & Import**
2. Tap **Export**, choose what to include, and save the file (e.g. to iCloud Drive or Files)
3. On your **new device**, install Read & Preach from the App Store
4. Open **Settings → Export & Import → Import**
5. Select your exported file — all your data will be restored

## Uninstalling

Press and hold the Read & Preach icon on your home screen → tap **Remove App** → **Delete App**.

> **Important:** Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data from the device. Make sure to export your data first if you want to keep it.
