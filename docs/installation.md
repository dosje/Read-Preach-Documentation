# Installation

## iOS (iPhone & iPad)

### Requirements
- iOS 14.0 or later
- Approximately 80 MB free storage

### Steps
1. Open the **App Store** on your device
2. Tap the **Search** icon and type "Read & Preach"
3. Locate the official Read & Preach app (by dosje)
4. Tap **Get**, then authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
5. The app will download and appear on your home screen

### Updating
To update to the latest version:
1. Open the App Store
2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
3. Scroll down and tap **Update** next to Read & Preach

---

## Android

### Requirements
- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
- Approximately 60 MB free storage

### Steps
1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your device
2. Search for "Read & Preach"
3. Tap the official Read & Preach listing
4. Tap **Install**
5. Accept any permissions requested and wait for the download to complete

### Updating
1. Open the Google Play Store
2. Tap the hamburger menu → **My apps & games**
3. Find Read & Preach and tap **Update**

---

## Downloading Content for Offline Use

To use Read & Preach without an internet connection:

1. Open the app and go to **Settings → Downloads**
2. Select the Bible translations or content packs you want to download
3. Tap **Download** — content will be available offline once complete

---

## Uninstalling

### iOS
Press and hold the app icon → Tap **Remove App** → **Delete App**

### Android
Go to **Settings → Apps → Read & Preach** → **Uninstall**

> **Note:** Uninstalling the app will remove locally downloaded content, but your account data, notes, and highlights are safely stored in the cloud and will be restored when you reinstall.
