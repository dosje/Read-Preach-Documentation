# Features

## Report Tab

The Report tab is your daily ministry log. It tracks everything you need for your monthly service report.

### Daily Entry

For each day you can record:
- **Hours** — actual time spent in ministry
- **Planned hours** — time planned ahead
- **Placements** — literature or magazines shared
- **Bible studies** — conducted studies
- **Comments** — personal notes for the day

Tap a day in the calendar to switch between days. All data is saved automatically as you type.

### Calendar

The calendar shows the current month with visual indicators on days that have data. You can:
- Tap any day to view or edit that day's entry
- Swipe left/right to navigate between months
- Tap the month/year title to jump to any month quickly
- Tap **Today** to return to the current date

On iPhone the calendar can be collapsed to a compact week view. On iPad it is always visible — as a sidebar in landscape, or above the form in portrait.

### Monthly Summary

Below the daily form, a summary card shows:
- Total hours this month vs. your monthly goal
- Hours remaining to reach your goal
- Total placements and Bible studies for the month
- Year-to-date totals and yearly goal

### Role Management

In **Settings → Report Settings** you can configure your ministry role:

- **Recurring role** — set Publisher, Auxiliary Pioneer, or Regular Pioneer with a start month and optional end month. Each role carries a different monthly hour goal.
- **One-time assignment** — set a temporary Auxiliary Pioneer month (15 or 30 hours) for a single month without changing your recurring role.
- **Role history** — a log of all past role changes.

## Ministry Timer

The Ministry Timer tracks how long you spend in field ministry and lets you save the time directly to your daily report.

### How to use

Tap the timer button in the Report tab to open the Ministry Timer:
- **Start** — begins timing when you enter the territory
- **Pause / Resume** — pause the timer during breaks and resume when you continue
- **Stop** — ends the session; a save dialog shows your time rounded to the nearest 30 minutes, ready to add to the day's report entry

### Home screen widgets

Add a Ministry Timer widget to your home screen. Two sizes are available:
- **Small** — shows the current timer with icon-only Pause/Resume and Stop controls
- **Medium** — shows the timer on the left with labelled Pause, Resume, and Stop buttons on the right

Both sizes update automatically and let you control the timer without opening the app.

### Live Activity & Dynamic Island

When you start the timer, a Live Activity is created automatically on supported devices:
- **Lock Screen** — shows the elapsed time with Pause/Resume and Stop controls
- **Dynamic Island** (iPhone 14 Pro and later) — shows a compact animated timer in the pill; long-press to expand and access controls

## Bible Tab

The Bible tab tracks your progress through all 1,189 chapters of the Bible.

### Progress Card

At the top of the Bible tab, the Progress card shows:
- A circular progress indicator with the percentage of chapters read
- Total chapters read out of 1,189
- Estimated completion date based on your reading plan
- Current reading streak (if the streak feature is enabled)
- A **View History** link to open your full reading history

### Today's Reading

If you have an active reading plan, a highlighted section shows today's assigned chapters. Each chapter card has:
- A checkbox to mark it as read (tap)
- A link button to open the chapter on JW.org (tap the arrow icon)

### Book List and Chapter Grid

The left column lists all Bible books with their completion percentage. Tap a book to open its chapter grid on the right. In the chapter grid:
- **Blue with checkmark** — chapter read
- **Green outline** — today's assigned chapter
- **Grey** — unread chapter

Tap a chapter to mark it read or unread. Long-press (or use the context menu) to open it in JW.org or JW Library.

### Reading Plans

Available reading modes:
- **Sequential** — Genesis to Revelation in order
- **NT → OT** — New Testament first, then Old Testament
- **Custom** — drag and drop books into your own preferred order
- **Free mode** — no daily schedule; mark chapters at your own pace

Available durations: 1 year, 2 years, 3 years, or a custom chapters-per-day rate (up to 10 per day).

If you fall behind, the app automatically reschedules missed chapters into your upcoming days while keeping the same daily pace.

### Reading Streak

The streak counter tracks how many consecutive days you have read at least one chapter.

- A **2-day grace period** applies before the streak resets — if you miss a day, the streak stays active for two more days
- If the Daily Reminder is enabled, you receive a notification on the first and second missed day
- **Milestone celebrations** with confetti and haptic feedback appear at: 5, 14, 30, 90, 180, and 365 consecutive days
- A special full-screen celebration appears when you complete all 1,189 chapters

### Reading History

Tap **View History** in the Progress card to open the history sheet. It shows:
- **Bible completions** — a log of every date you finished all 1,189 chapters (for tracking progress across multiple read-throughs over the years)
- **Daily reading log** — every chapter you have read, grouped by date (newest first), in Bible order

### Memorial Tracker

During the Memorial season (approximately one week before and after Nisan 14), a Memorial card and daily reading section appear. These show the assigned passages from the Bible and the book *Jesus — The Way, the Truth, the Life* for each day leading up to and following the Memorial.

- Tap a passage to mark it as read
- Tap the link icon to open the passage on JW.org or in JW Library
- The Memorial card shows your overall progress through all passages
- The tracker resets automatically each year after the Memorial date

## Settings

### General Settings

- **Language** — English, German, or French. Changing the language restarts the app.
- **Haptic feedback** — enable or disable vibration feedback when tapping
- **App icon** — choose from Default, Green, Gold, Purple, or Red

### Report Settings

- **Date format** — choose how dates are displayed, with a live preview
- **Recurring role** — configure your ongoing ministry role and monthly hour goal
- **One-time assignment** — add a temporary pioneer month
- **Role history** — view and manage past role entries

### Bible Reading Settings

- **Bible language** — choose the language for JW.org links (defaults to app language)
- **Reading mode** — sequential, NT→OT, custom, or free
- **Reading duration** — 1, 2, 3 years or custom chapters per day
- **Custom book order** — drag books to reorder (available in Custom mode)
- **Generate plan / Recalculate** — create or regenerate your reading schedule
- **Daily reminder** — set a push notification time; the notification lists today's unread chapters
- **Reading streak** — toggle the streak tracker on or off

### Memorial Tracker Settings

- **Enable Memorial Tracker** — show or hide the Memorial feature
- **Always show** — show the Memorial card outside the seasonal window
- **Memorial reminder** — set a daily notification during the Memorial season

### Export & Import

All your data can be exported to a JSON file and imported on another device or after reinstalling:
- Choose what to include: Ministry Reports, Bible Reading (chapters, plan, streak, history), Memorial Reading
- Export saves a file you can store anywhere (iCloud Drive, Files app, email, etc.)
- Import restores all selected data from a previously exported file

## Privacy & Data

- **All data is stored locally on your device** — nothing is sent to any server
- **No account required** — the app works fully offline from first launch
- **No tracking or analytics** — no data is collected or shared with third parties
- **Export/Import** is the only way to transfer data between devices
