# Features

Read & Preach is packed with tools to support your reading, study, and preaching journey.

## Scripture Reader

- Access multiple Bible translations
- Offline reading support — no internet required once downloaded
- Night mode for comfortable reading in low light
- Adjustable font size and style
- Verse-by-verse navigation

## Highlighting & Annotations

- Highlight verses in multiple colors
- Add personal notes to any passage
- Tag and categorize your notes
- Search across all your annotations

## Reading Plans

- Choose from curated daily reading plans
- Track your progress automatically
- Set daily reminders to stay on schedule
- Create your own custom reading plans

## Sermon Builder

- Create and organize sermon outlines
- Attach scripture references directly to sermon points
- Add speaker notes and illustrations
- Export sermons to PDF or share via email

## Study Tools

- Access cross-references for any verse
- Built-in concordance and word search
- Commentary integration (select translations)
- Original language tools (Hebrew/Greek)

## Community & Sharing

- Share verses and insights to social media
- Share sermon outlines with your congregation
- Bookmark content to revisit later

## Notifications & Reminders

- Daily verse notification
- Reading plan reminders
- Customizable notification schedule
